# NNNN — Title (a decision, stated as a fact)

**Status:** Proposed | Accepted | Superseded by NNNN · **Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Deviates from:** the skill principle or default practice this decision consciously trades away — or "nothing; recorded as a one-way door"

## Context

The forces at play: workload reality, constraints, and why the default answer doesn't fit here. Two or three sentences.

## Decision

What we will do, in the imperative. One paragraph.

## Alternatives considered

The credible options and the one-line reason each lost. A decision with no alternatives wasn't designed, it was assumed.

## Consequences

What this buys and what it costs — both, honestly. Include the compensating controls that make the cost acceptable.

## Revisit when

The concrete trigger events that reopen this decision. "Never" is not a trigger; if none exists, say what evidence would change our mind.
